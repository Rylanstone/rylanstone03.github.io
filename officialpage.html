Overview

Who I am, what I stand for, and what you can expect.

I am a twenty-one-year-old native of Draper, Utah, and an honors graduate from Corner Canyon High School. Number three of four children, I grew up learning the value of hard work, respect, honesty, and integrity. As a child, I developed a quick wit and a vivid sense of curiosity that has remained with me into adulthood. During my time in high school, I participated in various service activities, leadership groups, and team sports. Notable memberships include the Parent Teacher Student Association, Peer Leadership Team, and the Draper City Mayor's Youth Council. I also participated in the Boy Scouts of America program where I received the highest honor possible, the title of Eagle Scout. Through my involvement in these programs, I discovered a deeper love for service, the value of hard work, and the importance of detailed planning when trying to reach a goal.
